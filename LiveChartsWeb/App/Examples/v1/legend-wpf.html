Chart Legend

A legend contains a list of the series appearing in the chart, you can place it at Left, Bottom, Top or Right.

All you need to place a legend is set the LegendLocation property to a certain position 

<PieChart LegendLocation="Right"></lvc:PieChart>

You can also create your own legends control, All you need to do is create a new user control that inherits from LiveCharts.ChartLegend, you can also see included chart legend to help you build your own.

After your legend is ready you need to set it to your chart: 

myChart.ChartLegend = new myLegendControl();
