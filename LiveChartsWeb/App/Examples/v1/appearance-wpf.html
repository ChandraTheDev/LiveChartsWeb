Styling

A chart from LiveCharts inherits from WPF User Control, this means that you can style a chart practically as you normally would with any other control, like setting a Background, a Font or Horizontal alignment, in this article we are only going to focus on changing the unique properties from this library. Next image shows the chart we are building.

Background

This property is inherited from WPF UserControl, 

lineChart1.Background = new SolidColorBrush(Color.FromRgb(55, 55, 55));
<lvc:LineChart Background="#555555"></lvc:LineChart>
