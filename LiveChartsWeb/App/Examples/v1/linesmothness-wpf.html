LineSmoothness Property

The LineSmoothness property allows you to control the style of your line, This property goes from 0 to 1.

Concider this chart as an example 

<lvc:LineChart LineSmoothness="0">
  <lvc:LineChart.Series>
    <lvc:LineSeries Values="1, 5, 8" />
    <lvc:LineSeries Values="3, 6, 2" />
  </lvc:LineChart.Series>
</lvc:LineChart>

The image to the right has a LineSmoothness of 0, the one at left is 1, you can use any mid value according to your needs, default is 0.8.
